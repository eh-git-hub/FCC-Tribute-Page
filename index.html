Who is Satoshi Nakamoto?

Satoshi Nakamoto is the name used by the presumed pseudonym person or people whp developed bitcoin white paper, and created and deployed bitcoin's original reference implementation.


As part of the implementation, Nakamoto also devised the first blockchain database.


Nakamoto was active in the development of bitcoin up until December 2010.
